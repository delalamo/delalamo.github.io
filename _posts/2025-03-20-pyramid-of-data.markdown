Raw and partially processed data are fenced off from where they need to appear.

The vast majority of publications are downloadable as PDFs and render images as static PNGs (or equivalent file formats). That was understandable thirty years ago, as web technologies were not nearly as advanced as they are now. Yet even today, publications routinely host data on Zenodo in a way that is fully disconnected from the main text itself, adding unnecessary steps to data verification and reproducibility.
